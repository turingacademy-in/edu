Our Courses

Our courses are designed to blend foundational concepts with hands-on experience in AI, coding, and technology. Each program is curated to equip learners with practical skills for real-world success.

Beginner Tutorials

Duration: 8 hours

Jumpstart your coding journey with Turing Academy’s Beginner Tutorials for Grade 6! In just 8 hours, students will build their first mobile apps using MIT App Inventor—no prior experience needed. Through hands-on projects like designing interfaces and debugging games, young innovators learn computational thinking while having fun. Perfect for aspiring techies ready to create, test, and share their own apps!

Computation Thinking

Duration: 10 hours

Unlock computational thinking with Turing Academy’s *10-hour course* for Grade 7! Using MIT App Inventor, students design apps like a Piano Player, Maze Game, and Community Tour Guide—learning to decompose problems, reuse code, and test solutions. Through projects like ‘Sketch & Guess’ (multiplayer drawing apps) and a Final Community Project, they’ll master real-world skills while having fun. Perfect for young innovators ready to build, collaborate, and think like engineers!

The Internet of Things

Duration: 10 hours

Dive into the world of smart devices with Turing Academy’s *10-hour IoT course* for Grade 8! Using MIT App Inventor, students will connect sensors, build interactive apps, and control real-world objects—from smart lights to environmental monitors. Through projects like weather stations and motion-activated systems, they’ll learn to prototype IoT solutions while mastering wireless communication (Bluetooth/BLE) and data visualization. Perfect for young engineers ready to bridge the digital and physical worlds!

Data Science & Sensors

Duration: 10 hours

Unlock the power of data with Turing Academy’s *10-hour Data Science & Sensors* course for Grade 9! Students will collect real-time sensor data, visualize trends through interactive charts, and build predictive apps using MIT App Inventor. Projects include creating health monitors, environmental dashboards, and smart city models - blending coding, statistics, and real-world problem solving. Perfect for aspiring data scientists ready to turn numbers into impactful insights!
