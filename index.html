Our Courses

Our courses are designed to blend foundational concepts with hands-on experience in AI, coding, and technology. Each program is curated to equip learners with practical skills for real-world success.

Beginner Tutorial

Duration: 2 Weeks

Try out any of our beginner-friendly tutorials for grade 3-4—we suggest starting with 'Hello Codi!', but all are perfect for novice coders and can be completed in under an hour. Once you’ve built the starter apps, you can enhance them with new features or begin creating your own custom app designs.

Mobile CSP

Duration: 4 Weeks

This secondary school curriculum designed for grade 5-7 , endorsed by the College Board, offers AP Computer Science Principles with a focus on mobile computing. It features engaging, project-based learning that promotes collaboration and nurtures creativity among students.

AI with MIT App Inventor

Duration: 4 Weeks

Empower students to build smart mobile apps using AI-powered tools with MIT App Inventor. Designed for Grades 9–10, this course blends creativity with real-world technology skills.

Data Science & Sensors

Duration: 4 Weeks

Designed for grade 11-12 students, the Data Science & Sensors course provides an advanced exploration of IoT systems and data analytics. Students collect real-world sensor data, analyze trends using statistical methods, and build interactive apps to solve complex problems.
